Checked Exceptions

Checked exceptions are the child classes of the Exception class. Compiler will force us to handle the exception. These exceptions occur during the compile time of the program.

Examples: IO Exception, SQLException

Unchecked Exceptions

Unchecked exception occurs at the time of execution. These are also called as Runtime Exceptions. Runtime exceptions are ignored at the time of compilation.

Examples: Arithmetic Exception, NullPointer Exception, Out Of Bounds Exception(ArrayIndex out Of Bounds, string Out Of Bounds), Number Format Exception.
